A Legacy of Excellence Since 1892

Founded as a small liberal arts college, Horizon University has grown into one of the nation's most respected institutions of higher learning.

Established in 1892 by visionary educators and community leaders, Horizon University began with just three buildings and 120 students. Over the decades, we've expanded our campus, broadened our academic offerings, and built a reputation for excellence in teaching, research, and service.

Today, our university is home to more than 25,000 students and 1,200 faculty members across 12 colleges and schools. We remain committed to our founding principles of academic excellence, innovation, and inclusivity as we prepare the next generation of leaders to address the challenges of a changing world.

Mission, Vision, and Values

Our commitment to excellence drives everything we do at Horizon University.

Mission

To cultivate intellectual growth, creativity, and ethical leadership through exceptional teaching, innovative research, and dedicated service to our community and the world.

Vision

To be a transformative force in higher education, recognized globally for our innovative approach to learning, impactful research, and commitment to addressing society's most pressing challenges.

Values

  • Excellence in all endeavors
  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Diversity and inclusion
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Community engagement
